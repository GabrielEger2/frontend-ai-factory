Structural follow-ups
---------------------
1. `variants/` directories: CLAUDE.md describes them as optional ("same
   component, different density/color mode"), but 0 of 43 components have one.
   Decision needed: drop the convention from CLAUDE.md, or seed dense/dark
   variants for 2-3 high-value components (e.g. HeroSplitImage,
   CtaEditorialSplit, FaqAccordion).

2. layout/grid is over target (6 vs 4). PricingTiers, ProcessTimeline,
   LogoCloud, IconFeatureGrid arguably belong in their own categories
   (`pricing`, `process`, `partners`, `features`). Decision needed: raise the
   layout/grid target in CLAUDE.md, or split these into dedicated categories
   matching how their `purpose[]` already tags them.
